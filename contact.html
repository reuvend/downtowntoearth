OUR TEAM

Downtown to Earth was founded on October 12th, 2021 by Lauro Frauke
and Andria Berahtrham. Over the years, we have grown our team with the
goal to represent the diverse culture we have here in Houston. We are
always looking for people who are just as passionate about sustainable
agriculture as we are to help this organization expand as far as we can.
If you're interested in joining our team or have any questions about
Downtown to Earth, we are always available and open to working with you!
